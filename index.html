Why ModelAny

Less copying. More comparing.

少一些复制粘贴，多一些视角对比。

One prompt, many models

Type your question once and send it to every AI you select — no more pasting the same text into separate tabs.

输入一次，同时发给所有选中的模型，不再反复复制粘贴。

Compare perspectives faster

See how different models approach the same question, side by side. Find better answers in a single round.

同一个问题，多种模型思路并列呈现，一轮就能看到更多答案。

Open AI chats automatically

ModelAny opens a dedicated conversation tab for each selected model and fills in your prompt for you.

自动为每个选中的模型打开独立对话页面并填入问题。

Optional auto-send

Let ModelAny submit for you, or keep the prompt filled so you can review before sending. You stay in control.

可选自动发送，也可以只填入文本，发送前手动确认。

Keep conversations organized

Tabs opened in the same round are grouped together, so your workspace stays tidy across multiple models.

同一轮打开的标签页自动归组，多模型对话井然有序。

Local-first privacy

Drafts, settings, and history stay in your browser. ModelAny does not upload your prompts to its own server.

草稿、设置与记录保存在本地，不上传到 ModelAny 服务器。

Frequently asked questions

常见问题

What is ModelAny? ModelAny 是什么？

ModelAny is a free, open-source Chrome extension that lets you type one prompt and send it to several AI models at the same time, so you can compare their answers side by side.

ModelAny 是一款免费开源的 Chrome 扩展，输入一次提示词即可同时发送给多个 AI 模型，方便横向比较回答。

Which AI models does ModelAny support? ModelAny 支持哪些 AI 模型？

ModelAny supports eight models: ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Kimi, GLM, Qwen, Doubao, and Wenxin.

支持 8 个模型：ChatGPT、Gemini、DeepSeek、Kimi、GLM、通义千问、豆包、文心。

Is ModelAny free to use? ModelAny 免费吗？

Yes. ModelAny is free and open source. You can install it from GitHub and review all of the source code.

ModelAny 完全免费且开源，可从 GitHub 安装并查看全部代码。

Does ModelAny store my prompts? ModelAny 会保存我的提示词吗？

No. ModelAny is local-first — drafts, settings, and history stay in your browser, and prompts are sent only to the AI services you choose. ModelAny does not upload your prompts to its own server.

采用本地优先设计，草稿、设置与历史保存在浏览器本地，提示词只发送到你主动选择的 AI，不会上传到 ModelAny 服务器。

How do I install ModelAny? 如何安装 ModelAny？

Download the latest release from GitHub, unzip it, open chrome://extensions, enable Developer mode, and click “Load unpacked” to select the unzipped folder.

从 GitHub 下载最新版本并解压，打开 chrome://extensions，开启开发者模式，点击“加载已解压的扩展程序”并选择解压后的文件夹。

Can I compare AI answers with ModelAny? 可以用 ModelAny 比较 AI 的回答吗？

Yes — that is the core idea. ModelAny opens a separate tab for each selected model, fills in your prompt, and optionally sends it, so you can compare responses in one round.

可以，这正是核心功能。ModelAny 为每个选中的模型打开独立标签页并填入提示词，可选自动发送，一轮即可比较多个回答。

Does ModelAny submit my prompt automatically? ModelAny 会自动发送提示词吗？

It is optional. Enable auto-send to submit automatically, or leave it off so the prompt is filled in for you to review and send manually.

可选。开启自动发送会自动提交；关闭则只填入文本，由你手动确认发送。
