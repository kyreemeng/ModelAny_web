Frequently asked questions

What is ModelAny?

ModelAny is a free, open-source Chrome extension that lets you type one prompt and send it to several AI models at the same time, so you can compare their answers side by side.

Which AI models does ModelAny support?

ModelAny supports eight models: ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Kimi, GLM, Qwen, Doubao, and Wenxin.

Is ModelAny free to use?

Yes. ModelAny is free and open source. You can install it from GitHub and review all of the source code.

Does ModelAny store my prompts?

No. ModelAny is local-first — drafts, settings, and history stay in your browser, and prompts are sent only to the AI services you choose. ModelAny does not upload your prompts to its own server.

How do I install ModelAny?

Download the latest release from GitHub, unzip it, open chrome://extensions, enable Developer mode, and click “Load unpacked” to select the unzipped folder.

Can I compare AI answers with ModelAny?

Yes — that is the core idea. ModelAny opens a separate tab for each selected model, fills in your prompt, and optionally sends it, so you can compare responses in one round.

Does ModelAny submit my prompt automatically?

It is optional. Enable auto-send to submit automatically, or leave it off so the prompt is filled in for you to review and send manually.
