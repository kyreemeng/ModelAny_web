Frequently asked questions

Does this page publish an absolute ranking?

No. Conditional findings are published only when test conditions, raw outputs, and a review method are disclosed.

Are pricing and plan details definitive?

This page links to official sources and verification dates. Confirm the provider price shown for your region before purchasing.

Does ModelAny store prompts?

ModelAny is local-first. Prompts are sent only to the AI services you choose and are not uploaded to ModelAny servers.
